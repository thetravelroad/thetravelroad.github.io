Privacy Policy

Last updated on 05 June, 2015

Policy purpose

TheTravelRoad App (“TheTravelRoad”) provides this Privacy Policy to inform you of our policies and procedures for the collection, use and disclosure of personal information we receive from users of thetravelroad.in (this “Site”). This Privacy Policy only applies to information you provide to us through the Site.

This Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We will not reduce your rights under this Privacy Policy without your consent. We will post all privacy policy changes on this page thetravelroad.in/privacy. If changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice (including, for certain services, email notification of privacy policy changes).

Information we collect

We will create your Account based upon the Personal Information you provide, and you will become a Member of TheTravelRoad. Your Personal Information will be used to provide our service. TheTravelRoad collects and stores all information provided by its visitors and members. This includes, but is not limited to: registration information, profile activity, IP addresses, referral pages, service behavior, and traffic data. We will not log your information or behavior on other third party applications unrelated to TheTravelRoad.

All Members may review, update, correct or delete Personal Information in their Account by editing such profile via the Site. If you completely delete all such information, then your Account may become deactivated. We will make efforts to honor your request. We may retain an archived copy of your records as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

Your data

Your data serves the following functions:

  • 1. Allows TheTravelRoad to better understand Site usage and subsequently improve content, functionality, and features of the Site.
  • 2. Information you provide to TheTravelRoad may be used for Customer Service, Accounting, and New Product Promotion. TheTravelRoad may use aggregate or anonymous information which will not be linked to identified individuals for various other uses for itself and third parties.

External links

Our Site contains links to external sites. If you choose to purchase a product by "clicking on" a product on TheTravelRoad you will be directed to other websites. We do not take responsibility for cookies or other files that could be placed on your computer by those websites. We encourage you to read their privacy policy if you have concerns with their behavior.

Membership

When you register as a member of TheTravelRoad, you are agreeing to receive emails and in-system notices for announcements, newsletters, recommendations and policy updates. If you would like to opt out of our emails, please contact the privacy officer at [email protected], or follow up unsubscribe link on those emails.

TheTravelRoad reserves the right to delete your account without notice, for legitimate business reasons. In the unlikely even that this occurs, we will provide an email notification with further details.

Membership termination

If you would like your public profile and membership with TheTravelRoad terminated, log in, go to your profile and click the "Edit" button. Then follow the "Delete my account" link.

The Public and the Community

Non members (the “Public”) and members of TheTravelRoad will be able to view your public profile, including: your first name, surname, TheTravelRoad username, personal website, users you follow, followers, liked items, and number of items liked.

If you have any questions about this Policy or any of the context described, please contact us at [email protected]
